AI-assisted Compton-camera reconstruction for ²²⁵Ac imaging

An end-to-end imaging pipeline for a liquid-xenon Compton camera built for targeted alpha therapy: GEANT4 Monte Carlo simulation, GPU-accelerated LM-OSEM reconstruction, kernel-density-estimated system matrices, and Bayesian optimisation of event-selection cuts. A scale-free quality score over the steep region of an estuarine salinity field, used to benchmark seven greedy acquisition functions against a differential-evolution global optimiser on top of a Gaussian-process surrogate. The optimiser hits DQS = 0.937 at N = 20 while the strongest greedy heuristic stalls at 0.675 — a structural gap of about 28% of attainable quality.
